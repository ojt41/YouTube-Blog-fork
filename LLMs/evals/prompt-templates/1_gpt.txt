# Prompt Template for Blog Post Generation

## Instructions for GPT-4o-mini:

You are tasked with generating a blog post from a YouTube transcript. The blog post should mimic the style, tone, and formatting of the blog posts found in the "blogs" folder. Follow these guidelines:

1. **Title and Subtitle**: 
   - Start with a catchy and informative title.
   - Follow with a subtitle that provides a brief overview or intriguing question related to the content.

2. **Introduction**:
   - Begin with a hook that captures the reader's attention.
   - Provide a brief overview of the topic and what the reader can expect to learn.

3. **Main Content**:
   - Break down the content into clear, concise sections with headings.
   - Use bullet points or numbered lists where appropriate to enhance readability.
   - Include relevant examples, anecdotes, or case studies to illustrate key points.
   - Use images or diagrams to support the text, if applicable (describe them if not possible to include).

4. **Conclusion**:
   - Summarize the key takeaways from the post.
   - End with a call to action or a thought-provoking question to engage the reader further.

5. **Formatting**:
   - Use markdown for headings, lists, and emphasis.
   - Ensure the post is well-structured and easy to read.

## Example Structure:

### Title: [Insert Title Here]
#### Subtitle: [Insert Subtitle Here]

[Write an engaging introduction that sets the stage for the topic.]

### [Section Heading]

[Discuss the main points of this section. Use examples and anecdotes.]

### [Another Section Heading]

[Continue with additional sections as needed, following the same format.]

* * *

### Conclusion

[Summarize the main points and provide a closing thought or call to action.]

* * *

## Transcript:

{transcript}

## Additional Notes:
- Ensure the tone is consistent with the existing blog posts: informative, engaging, and accessible.
- Maintain a balance between technical details and general readability.
- Use hyperlinks to relevant resources or references where applicable.
